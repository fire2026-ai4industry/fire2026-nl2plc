Eligibility

The shared task is open to:

  • Students
  • Researchers
  • Academicians
  • Industry Professionals

Team Size

A team may consist of up to 4 members. Individual participation is also allowed.

Participation Rules

  • Only open-source models are allowed.
  • Only open-source LLMs/SLMs with up to 8B parameters are permitted.
  • Teams must submit original work.
  • Multiple teams from the same organization are permitted.

Dataset Access

The dataset will be made available only to registered participants. Access instructions will be shared after successful registration.
