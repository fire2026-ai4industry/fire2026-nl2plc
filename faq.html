Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Who can participate?

Students, researchers, academicians, and industry practitioners are welcome to participate.

What is the maximum team size?

A team can consist of up to 4 members.

Do I need to register before accessing the dataset?

Yes. Dataset access will be provided only to registered participants.

Can I participate individually?

Yes. Individual participation is allowed.

Are commercial or proprietary LLMs allowed?

No. Only open-source models are permitted.

Can external datasets be used?

No.

Will baseline systems be provided?

Details regarding baseline systems will be announced later.

How will submissions be evaluated?

Evaluation will consider syntax correctness and structural similarity performance depending on the task.

Where can I ask additional questions?

Please contact the organizing committee through the official email address once announced.
